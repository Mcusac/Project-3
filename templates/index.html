Graphical Depictions of Baseball Player Statistics

This project is meant to display statistics for both pitching and batting of the given player based on their player ID and the year in which they played. From these inputs, the user will be able to both see their statistics and as well as visualizations of these statistics.

Each of the tabs above are named accordingly for finding what you are looking for. From there you can input the desired player (using the players "playerID") and if needed the year in which that player had played. Using This information, the webpage will display a total of 4 graphs tailored to your selected player.
